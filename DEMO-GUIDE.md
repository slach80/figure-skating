## 🎯 Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How long to implement?**
A: 12-16 weeks for full system with all features.

**Q: Can we keep using SportsEngine during transition?**
A: Yes, we can run both in parallel during migration.

**Q: What about payment processing?**
A: Integrates with Stripe, Square, or existing processor.

**Q: Who maintains it?**
A: Small monthly retainer for support, or train club staff.

**Q: Can we modify it later?**
A: Yes, fully customizable unlike SportsEngine.

**Q: What about mobile?**
A: Fully responsive, works perfectly on phones/tablets.

**Q: Data migration?**
A: We export from SportsEngine and import to new system.

**Q: Training required?**
A: 2-3 hours for staff, parents learn in minutes.
