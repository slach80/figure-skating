Meet Our Coaches

MC
Head Coach

Michelle Chen

Head Coach PSA Master Rated 20+ Years

Michelle is our head coach and has been with Line Creek FSC since 2015. With PSA Master Rating and over 20 years of coaching experience, she has guided numerous skaters to regional and national success.

A former competitive skater herself, Michelle competed through the Senior level and brings a wealth of technical knowledge and competitive experience to her coaching. She specializes in advanced freestyle technique, competitive program development, and mental preparation.

Basic Skills

Sarah Thompson

Basic Skills Director PSA Rated 12 Years

Sarah directs our Learn to Skate USA program and specializes in working with beginning skaters of all ages. Her patient, encouraging teaching style helps new skaters build confidence and develop strong foundational skills.

With 12 years of coaching experience, Sarah has helped hundreds of skaters take their first steps on the ice and progress through the Basic Skills levels. She's passionate about making skating accessible and fun for everyone.

DP
Freestyle

David Park

Freestyle Specialist PSA Certified 8 Years

David brings elite competitive experience to Line Creek FSC. A former nationally competitive skater who landed double axel and triple jumps, he understands the technical demands and mental challenges of competitive skating.

Since transitioning to coaching, David has focused on helping skaters master difficult technical elements, particularly jumps. His methodical approach breaks down complex skills into achievable steps, helping skaters progress safely and confidently.

Specialties:

  • ✓ Jump Technique (All Levels)
  • ✓ Double & Triple Jumps
  • ✓ Freestyle Pre-Juvenile - Senior
  • ✓ Competition Strategy

Achievements:
