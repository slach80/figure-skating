About Line Creek FSC

Building champions on and off the ice since 2010

Our Story

We are an Ice Skating Institute and US Figure Skating affiliated club, dedicated to the love of skating through encouragement and advancement. Our programs include individual skating and synchronized skating.

We skate at the Line Creek Community Center in Kansas City, MO, and are a proud member club of the Mid-West Figure Skating Council.

Our club has grown to include the KC MOMENTUM Synchronized Skating Teams, featuring over 90 athletes across 9 different teams, ranging from age 3 to adult.

Our Affiliations

US Figure Skating

As a USFS member club, we follow the highest standards in figure skating instruction and competition. Our skaters have access to test tracks, sanctioned competitions, and national-level opportunities.

Member since: 2010

Ice Skating Institute

Our ISI affiliation provides recreational skaters with a fun, achievement-based program. Skaters can progress through ISI levels and participate in recreational competitions.

Member since: 2010
