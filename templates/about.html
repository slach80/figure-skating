About Line Creek FSC

Building champions on and off the ice since 2010

Our Story

Line Creek Figure Skating Club was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide a safe, supportive, and inspiring environment for skaters of all ages and abilities to pursue their dreams on the ice.

As both an Ice Skating Institute (ISI) and US Figure Skating (USFS) affiliated club, we offer comprehensive programming that serves recreational skaters through elite competitive athletes.

Located at the beautiful Line Creek Community Center in Kansas City, Missouri, our club has grown to become one of the premier figure skating destinations in the region.

⛸️
🎯

Our Mission

To empower skaters of all ages and skill levels through quality instruction, fostering athletic excellence, personal growth, and lifelong love of figure skating.

Our Vision

To be the leading figure skating club in the Kansas City region, recognized for developing confident, skilled skaters who excel both on and off the ice.

Our Affiliations

US Figure Skating

As a USFS member club, we follow the highest standards in figure skating instruction and competition. Our skaters have access to test tracks, sanctioned competitions, and national-level opportunities.

Member since: 2010

Ice Skating Institute

Our ISI affiliation provides recreational skaters with a fun, achievement-based program. Skaters can progress through ISI levels and participate in recreational competitions.

Member since: 2010
